The Best Movie According to Francis

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather (1972) – Directed by Francis Ford Coppola

This classic crime drama chronicles the powerful Italian-American mafia family of Don Vito Corleone. Its themes of power, loyalty, and family, along with unforgettable performances by Marlon Brando and Al Pacino, have made it a masterpiece of filmmaking.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) – Directed by Frank Darabont

Based on a Stephen King novella, this prison drama tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man wrongfully convicted of murder, and his journey toward hope and friendship with fellow inmate Red, played by Morgan Freeman. It's a film about resilience and the human spirit.

Inception (2010) – Directed by Christopher Nolan

This mind-bending science fiction thriller follows a team of thieves who infiltrate the subconscious to plant an idea in someone’s mind. With its stunning visual effects and intricate plot, Inception explores the nature of dreams, reality, and time.
